A set of six generalized densities:
1.	ldpi (low) ~120dpi
2.	mdpi (medium) ~160dpi
3.	hdpi (high) ~240dpi
4.	xhdpi (extra-high) ~320dpi
5.	xxhdpi (extra-extra-high) ~480dpi
6.	xxxhdpi (extra-extra-extra-high) ~640dpi
